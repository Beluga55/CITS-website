Features

These are the features for our "Know Your Future" website

Academic Advising

This application offers academic advisors who help students with course selection, academic planning, and exploring academic opportunities.

Libraries and Research Resources

This application provides access to millions of books, journals, and online resources through its extensive library system, tailored to students' specific fields of study.

Career Services

This app offers tutoring, career counseling, and internship support to help students excel academically and plan for their future.

Clubs

This app provides counseling, wellness programs, and resources for students' mental health and overall well-being.

Tutoring Services

This app offers online tutoring, study groups, and workshops to support students in various courses.

Support Services

This App provides support services for diverse student backgrounds, including counseling, advising, and resources tailored to their needs.

Religious and spiritual services

This app offers religious and spiritual resources to support students' diverse needs and foster a sense of community.

Online Music Services

This app offers diverse music courses, ensembles, and performance opportunities for students interested in music.

Innovation Challenge

This application hosts an annual competition to inspire students' innovative ideas and provides support to bring those ideas to life.
